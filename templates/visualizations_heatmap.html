Insights From This Visualization

  • CCTV cameras are not evenly distributed across the city of Baltimore. Many neighborhoods have no cameras at all.
  • There seems to be no consistent relationship between the presence of cameras and crime, as extensive criminal activity occurs in areas with and without cameras.
