Insights From This Visualization

  • There is no discernable relationship between population and number of CCTV cameras in a zip code. The most populated zip code, with almost 70k residents, has no cameras, while a zip code with just over 16k residents has over 100 cameras.
  • Zip codes with lower media incomes tend to have more CCTV cameras.
  • Zip codes with a higher proportion of non-white populations tend to have more CCTV cameras.
